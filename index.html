About MINEBLOCKY (The Blocky World)

**MINEBLOCKY** is your definitive single-player offline sandbox simulation, dedicated to the golden age of block-building and survival gaming. Explore infinite, procedurally generated worlds made entirely of textured cubes! The experience includes all your favorite survival mechanics, from resource gathering and crafting to fighting mobs and exploring deep caves.

Place and break blocks with ease using old-school block physics. Craft tools, armor, and elaborate structures. The world is pre-loaded with biomes to explore and hidden dungeons to conquer.
